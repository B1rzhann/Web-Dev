Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Milk 1.5 cups
Eggs 2
Butter 2 tbsp

Steps:

1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, milk, and eggs until smooth.

2. Heat a non-stick pan and melt the butter.

3. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan, cook for 2 minutes, then flip.

4. Repeat for the rest of the batter. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Minced beef 500g

Greek Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 3, diced
Cucumber 1, sliced
Feta cheese 200g, cubed
Olives 100g
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Steps:

1. Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, feta cheese, and olives in a large bowl.

2. Drizzle with olive oil and gently toss to mix.

3. Serve chilled as a side dish or light meal.
